# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in the Polygon Area Calculator project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, religion, gender identity, sexual identity, nationality, personal appearance, race, or level of experience.

We pledge to act in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

---

## Our Standards

### Examples of positive behavior:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language  
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints  
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully  
- Focusing on what is best for the community  
- Showing empathy and kindness toward others  

### Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment, insults, trolling, or personal attacks  
- Public or private harassment  
- Publishing others' private information  
- Conduct that is inappropriate in a professional setting  

---

## Our Responsibilities
Project maintainers must ensure acceptable behavior and may take corrective action including warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans for unacceptable conduct.

---

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to:
- GitHub repositories  
- Issue trackers  
- Pull requests  
- Discussions  
- Any project-associated spaces

## Enforcement Guidelines

### 1. Correction
**Impact:** Minor inappropriate behavior  
**Action:** Private reminder of expected behavior  

### 2. Warning
**Impact:** Single incident or repeated behavior  
**Action:** Official warning  

### 3. Temporary Ban
**Impact:** Serious violations  
**Action:** Temporary suspension  

### 4. Permanent Ban
**Impact:** Severe or repeated violations  
**Action:** Removal from community
